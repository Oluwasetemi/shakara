About Shakara

Shakara is a mobile application originally thought out and developed by Uyoyo Sule. The idea behind the application is to provide people looking for fashion ideas with a central place where they can find nice fashion ideas and get their Nigerian tailors to have them sewn. The word Shakara has is attributed to a sense of pride or show-off nature to with intent to overshadow others.

This app is 100% open source and contributions are welcome. However if you wan come use am make money, make sure say you call Madam Uyoyo and Oga Sani oo, Tomato don cost for market we wont mind some equity.
